Project Oasis: The Origin Story

From the fisrt boom with Cryptopunks and BAYC, to the insanse run of Goblintown, to Jack Butchers Checks. We have seen various highs and lows in this market but we have stayed in the market together. Why? Is it the potential to create wealth for yourself and those around you? Is it for the art? Is it for the community? We all have our reasons for being in this market but connecting and watching a project succeed is what keeps us in it. Connection. We as humans need this connection with one another in this era of becoming more and more digital. Art. We have shifted the meaning of what art is and how we create it in this digital era. Wealth. We all want some form of wealth whether that be building a name for yourself or being able to provide for your family without worry. These 3 words are what brings us into this space.
